The Good Life Festival is an exciting night designed to celebrate and promote healthy living! Enjoy an evening full of FREE activities, live DJ, Zumba and line dancing sessions, human foosball, photo booth, free snacks, glow-flow yoga, rock climbing, interactive well-being activity booths and much more!

Date: Saturday, May 2nd
Time: 7–11pm
Location: Town Square & Matthew’s Quad
Price: FREE for UCSD students
Wristbands: Bring your Student ID to receive a wristband! Attendees must sign a waiver to receive a wristband and partake in the Good Life Festival. Stay tuned for details on waiver sign up and wristband distribution!
Thank you for coming to the Good Life Festival!

The Good Life’s Mission
The Good Life Festival serves as a healthy, social opportunity for UC San Diego students. The Festival’s purpose is to engage students in fun, healthy on-campus activities and also inform students of on-campus well-being resources.
